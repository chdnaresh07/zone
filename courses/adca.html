This is an introductory course that does not presuppose any previous knowledge of computers.
You Will Study the use of basic application software and operating system in various business processes.
By the end of course each student will able to:
Go into a small company or business, analyze their Business Model and will be able to enter as well as process data using MS Office. Perform basic accounting functions like Voucher Entry, bill processing, etc. in Tally.
